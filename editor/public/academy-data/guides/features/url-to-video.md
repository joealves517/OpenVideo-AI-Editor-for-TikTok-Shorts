# URL to Video

Turn any webpage into a professional video - perfect for affiliate marketers and product promotion.

---

## What is URL to Video?

**Definition**: Scrapes images from any webpage and creates video automatically

**Perfect For**: Affiliate marketers, real estate agents, product promotion

**Key Benefit**: **No credits for image scraping** - only pay for video generation

---

## How It Works

### The Process

1. Paste URL of webpage
2. OpenVideo analyzes page
3. Extracts images and content
4. Creates video automatically

**Result**: Professional promotional video from any URL

---

## Complete Workflow

### Step 1: Access Feature

**Where to Find**:
- In Faceless Video Generator
- Select "URL to Video" style
- Paste URL

---

### Step 2: Generate Script

**How**:
1. Click "Generate Script from URL"
2. AI analyzes page
3. Creates complete script
4. Ready to use

**What You Get**:
- Complete script based on page content
- SEO-optimized
- Value-focused
- Ready to use

---

### Step 3: Create Video

**Process**:
1. Follow normal video creation
2. Images already scraped
3. No credit cost for images
4. Only pay for video generation

**Credits**: Only video generation credits (13 per minute)

**Result**: Professional video with no image credit cost.
