# Idea Discovery - Research-Backed Content

Use real-time data to find viral topics and create content that performs.

---

## What is Idea Discovery?

**Definition**: Research tool that pulls insights from real-time third-party data sources

**Why It's Powerful**: Unlike AI-generated ideas, this uses actual search data, trends, and performance metrics

**Result**: Content based on real audience demand, not guesswork

---

## How It Works

### The Technology

**Data Sources**:
- Google (search questions, volume)
- Instagram (trending hashtags)
- YouTube (top performing video titles)

**What You Get**:
- 10 high-quality topic results per search
- Search volume data
- Trend direction
- Cost per click (CPC)
- Competition level

**Cost**: 15 credits per search

---

## Using Idea Discovery

### Step 1: Access the Feature

**Where to Find**:
- Left sidebar navigation
- Click "Idea Discovery"
- Or from dashboard

---

### Step 2: Select Your Source

**Options**:
- **Google**: Most search questions
- **Instagram**: Trending hashtags
- **YouTube**: Top performing video titles

**Choose Based On**:
- Your primary platform
- Content type
- Research needs

---

### Step 3: Enter Your Keyword

**What to Enter**:
- Broad topic (e.g., "AI content creation")
- Niche keyword
- Content area
- Target audience interest

**Best Practices**:
- Be specific but not too narrow
- Use your niche keywords
- Test different variations

---

### Step 4: Review Results

**What You Get**:
- 10 topic results
- Search volume
- Trend direction (rising/falling)
- Competition level
- CPC data

**How to Use**:
- Review all results
- Identify high-volume, low-competition topics
- Select winners
- Create content

---

### Step 5: Create Content Instantly

**Quick Action**:
- Click "Create Faceless" or "Create Avatar" beside any topic
- Goes directly to video creation
- Topic pre-filled
- Start creating immediately

**Result**: Research to content in seconds.

---

## Pro Tips

### 1. Research Before Creating

**Strategy**: Use Idea Discovery before creating content

**Why**: Data-driven topics perform better

**Result**: Higher-performing content.

---

### 2. Target Long-Tail Keywords

**Strategy**: Look for 3-5 word phrases

**Why**: Easier to rank, less competition

**Result**: Better search rankings.

---

### 3. Track Your Searches

**Feature**: All searches saved automatically

**Benefit**: Revisit without spending credits

**Pro Tip**: Build library of researched topics.

---

### 4. Combine with Other Features

**Workflow**:
1. Research with Idea Discovery
2. Create with Faceless Generator
3. Schedule with Bulk Scheduler
4. Track with Analytics

**Result**: Complete content workflow.

---

## Best Practices

### 1. Regular Research

**Frequency**: Weekly or monthly

**Action**: Research topics for upcoming content

**Result**: Always have winning topics ready.

---

### 2. Analyze Trends

**What to Look For**:
- Rising trends (opportunity)
- Stable trends (evergreen)
- Falling trends (avoid)

**Result**: Strategic content planning.

---

### 3. Test Different Sources

**Strategy**: Use all three sources

**Why**: Different insights per platform

**Result**: Comprehensive research.
