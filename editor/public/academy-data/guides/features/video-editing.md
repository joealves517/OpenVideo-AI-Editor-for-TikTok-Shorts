# Video Editing Mastery

Complete guide to editing your videos in OpenVideo Editor for perfect results.

---

## Accessing the Video Editor

### How to Access

**Method 1: From My Videos**:
1. Go to "My Videos" section
2. Find video you want to edit
3. Click "Edit Images, Subtitles"
4. Editing panel opens

**Method 2: After Generation**:
1. Video appears in "My Videos"
2. Click "Edit" immediately
3. Make adjustments
4. Save changes

---

## Five Powerful Editing Options

### 1. Add Motion

**What It Does**: Animates existing images or replaces with motion-based scenes

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Edit Image"
2. Select "Add Motion"
3. Enter motion prompt (e.g., "make fire burn, stars move")
4. Generate motion
5. Save

**Credits**: 11 credits for 5 seconds

**Pro Tip**: Add motion to key moments for maximum impact.

---

### 2. Upload Image

**What It Does**: Bring in your own media

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Edit Image"
2. Select "Upload Image"
3. Choose file from device
4. Upload
5. Save

**Credits**: **FREE** - No credit cost

**Pro Tip**: Build library of custom images for reuse.

---

### 3. Image Regenerate

**What It Does**: Creates new image with new prompt

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Edit Image"
2. Select "Image Regenerate"
3. Enter new prompt
4. Review options
5. Select favorite
6. Save

**Credits**: 
- **First 3 regenerations**: FREE per video
- **Additional**: 1 credit each

**Pro Tip**: Use free regenerations wisely.

---

### 4. Stock Videos

**What It Does**: Access full stock video library

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Edit Image"
2. Select "Stock Videos"
3. Search keyword
4. Browse options
5. Select clip
6. Save

**Credits**: **FREE** - No credit cost

---

### 5. Asset Library

**What It Is**: Your personal collection of previously created content

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Edit Image"
2. Select "Asset Library"
3. Browse your assets
4. Select what you need
5. Save

**Credits**: **FREE** - No credit cost

**Pro Tip**: Asset library grows with every video - use it!

---

## Editing Subtitles

### Subtitle Customization

**What You Can Edit**:
- Subtitle text
- Font style
- Position (top, middle, bottom)
- Animation
- Colors

**Important Note**: Changing subtitle text won't affect voiceover. To update voice, edit script and regenerate video.

---

## Advanced Visual Controls

### Background Music

**Options Available**:
- New Wave 4 (popular)
- Various genres
- Mood-based selection
- Volume control

**Pro Tip**: Music should enhance, not distract.

---

### Video Filters

**What It Does**: Applies visual filters for style customization

**Pro Tip**: Use filters consistently for brand recognition.

## Saving Your Edits

**How to Save**:
1. Make all edits
2. Review changes
3. Click "Save Video"
4. New version created
5. Original preserved

**Export**: FREE after editing

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Edit Strategically**: Focus on key moments
2. **Build Asset Library**: Reuse content, save credits
3. **Use Free Options**: First 3 regenerations, asset library, stock videos
4. **Test and Learn**: See what works, iterate
