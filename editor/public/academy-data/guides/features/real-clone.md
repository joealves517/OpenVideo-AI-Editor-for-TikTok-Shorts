# Real Clone Feature

Create your own AI avatar and voice clone for personal branding without being on camera.

---

## What is Real Clone?

**Definition**: Create your personal AI avatar and voice clone

**Perfect For**: Creators who want to grow their channels but aren't comfortable being on camera all the time

**Result**: Personal branding without camera presence

---

## Three Clone Options

### 1. Real Clone Studio Avatar

**What It Is**: Requires green screen recording

**How It Works**:
- Record in front of green screen
- OpenVideo crops you out cleanly
- Overlay any background during editing
- Studio quality look

**Best For**: Professional, polished appearance

---

### 2. Real Clone Light Avatar

**What It Is**: Keeps original background from footage

**How It Works**:
- Film in setting you're comfortable showing
- Original background maintained
- No green screen needed
- Natural appearance

**Best For**: Authentic, natural look

---

### 3. Voice Cloning

**What It Is**: Clone your voice for use in videos

**How It Works**:
- Record sample audio
- OpenVideo creates voice clone
- Use in all videos
- Available on all plans

**Best For**: Consistent voice across all content

**Cost**: **FREE** - Available on all plans at no extra cost

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Add Basic Info

**What's Needed**:
- Basic information
- Account details
- Preferences

---

### Step 2: Choose Recording Method

**Options**:
- Upload pre-recorded audio file
- Record directly inside OpenVideo using built-in voice recorder

**Pro Tip**: Record in quiet space for best quality.

---

### Step 3: Record Your Voice

**Using Built-in Recorder**:
1. Open voice recorder
2. Sample script provided
3. Read script aloud
4. Or speak naturally
5. Ensure clear audio

**Quality Tip**: Quality of voice clone depends entirely on quality of input - record in quiet space.

---

### Step 4: Submit for Review

**Process**:
1. Complete recording
2. Check consent box
3. Hit submit
4. Wait for review

**Timeline**: Within a few minutes, your voice will be reviewed, approved, and ready to use

---

## Using Your Clone

### In Video Creation

**How to Use**:
1. Create video as normal
2. Select your cloned voice
3. Generate video
4. Your voice throughout

**Result**: Consistent voice across all content.

---

## Pro Tips

### 1. Record High-Quality Audio

**Best Practices**:
- Quiet environment
- Clear speech
- Good microphone (if available)
- Natural pace

**Result**: Better voice clone quality.

---

### 2. Use Consistently

**Strategy**: Use your voice clone in all videos

**Why**: Builds brand recognition

**Result**: Consistent personal brand.

---

### 3. Test Different Recordings

**Strategy**: Try different scripts/styles

**Why**: Find what works best

**Result**: Optimal voice clone.
