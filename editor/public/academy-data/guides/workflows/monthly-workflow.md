# Monthly Bulk Workflow

Complete workflow for planning and creating a month of content in one day.

---

## Why Monthly Planning?

**The Benefits**:
- Plan entire month in advance
- Create all content at once
- Automatic publishing
- Stress-free consistency
- Better quality

**The Impact**: Month of content in 2-3 hours, automatic publishing

**Monthly planning can save 20+ hours per month.**

---

## Monthly Planning Process

### Step 1: Review Last Month (30 minutes)

**What to Review**:
- Top-performing content
- What worked
- What didn't work
- Engagement patterns
- Revenue performance

**Questions to Ask**:
- What got most views?
- What got most engagement?
- What converted best?
- What should I do more of?
- What should I stop doing?

**Result**: Data-driven planning for next month.

---

### Step 2: Research Topics (1 hour)

**How to Research**:
1. Use [OpenVideo Editor Idea Discovery](/)
2. Check trending topics
3. Analyze competitor content
4. Review saved topics
5. Identify gaps

**What to Find**:
- 30-60 topics for the month
- Mix of evergreen and trending
- Variety of formats
- High-demand topics

**Using [Idea Discovery](/)**:
- Research 10-15 keywords
- Get 100-150 topic ideas
- Select best 30-60
- Plan content calendar

**Result**: Month of content ideas ready.

---

### Step 3: Create Content Calendar (30 minutes)

**How to Create**:
1. Set up calendar (monthly view)
2. Assign topics to days
3. Plan content mix
4. Schedule optimal times
5. Add themes

**Tools**:
- [OpenVideo Editor Content Calendar](/)
- Google Calendar
- Spreadsheet
- Planning app

**Result**: Visual plan for entire month.

---

### Step 4: Batch Create Content (2-3 hours)

**Using Bulk Scheduler**:
1. Open Bulk Scheduler in [OpenVideo Editor](/)
2. Enter topics from calendar
3. Configure settings
4. Generate all videos
5. Schedule automatically

**Process**:
- Enter 30-60 topics
- Set posting schedule
- Configure video settings
- Generate all videos
- Automatic scheduling

**Result**: Month of content created and scheduled.

---

## Bulk Scheduler Workflow

### Step 1: Access Bulk Scheduler

**Where to Find**:
- Dashboard: "Bulk Creation" button
- Or from Faceless Video Generator: Toggle "Bulk Schedule"

---

### Step 2: Set Up Schedule

**Configure**:
- Number of videos to create
- Start date
- Duration (days)
- Posting frequency
- Videos per day
- Posting times

**Example**:
- 30 videos
- Start: Next month
- Duration: 30 days
- Frequency: 1 video per day
- Time: 9 AM daily

---

### Step 3: Configure Video Settings

**Settings**:
- Video style
- Voice actor
- Language
- Subtitle style
- Length
- Aspect ratio

**Pro Tip**: Use same settings for consistency.

---

### Step 4: Generate Scripts

**How**:
- Enter topic or keyword
- AI generates unique scripts
- One script per video
- All based on your topic

**Using AI Script Assistant**:
- Enter main topic
- AI creates variations
- Unique scripts
- Proven formulas

**Result**: 30 unique scripts ready.

---

### Step 5: Generate Videos

**Process**:
1. Review script summary
2. Check credit requirements
3. Click "Generate Videos"
4. Wait for processing
5. Videos scheduled automatically

**Timeline**: 1-2 hours for 30 videos

**Result**: Month of content created and scheduled.

## Monthly Content Mix

### Theme-Based Planning

**How It Works**:
- Plan monthly themes
- Weekly sub-themes
- Daily content supports themes
- Cohesive strategy

**Example**:
- Month: AI Tools for Creators
- Week 1: AI Script Writing
- Week 2: AI Video Creation
- Week 3: AI Automation
- Week 4: AI Optimization

**Benefit**: Cohesive content, builds authority.

---

### Content Mix

**Weekly Mix**:
- Monday: Educational (How-to)
- Tuesday: Tips (Quick tips)
- Wednesday: Entertainment (Fun facts)
- Thursday: Case Study
- Friday: Promotional (Product review)

**Benefit**: Audience variety, better engagement.

---

## Pro Tips

### 1. Plan Monthly, Execute Daily

**Strategy**:
- Plan entire month on first Sunday
- Create all content using Bulk Scheduler
- Execute daily with minimal effort
- Review and adjust weekly

**Result**: Stress-free content creation.

---

### 2. Use Themes

**Benefits**:
- Cohesive content
- Better SEO
- Series potential
- Authority building

**How**: Monthly themes, weekly sub-themes.

---

### 3. Mix Content Types

**Variety**:
- Educational
- Entertainment
- Promotional
- Inspirational
- Trending

**Result**: Engaged audience, better performance.

---

### 4. Leave Room for Trends

**Strategy**:
- Plan 80% in advance
- Leave 20% for trends
- Quick response to opportunities
- Balance planning and flexibility

**Result**: Best of both worlds.
