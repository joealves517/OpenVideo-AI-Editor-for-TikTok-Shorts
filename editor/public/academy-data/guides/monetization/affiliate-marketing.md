# Affiliate Marketing Strategy

Complete guide to building profitable affiliate marketing income.

---

## Why Affiliate Marketing Works

**The Opportunity**: 5-10x higher earning potential than AdSense

**The Reality**: $50-500 per 1,000 views (with good conversion)

**The Benefit**: No inventory, scalable, passive potential

---

## Finding Affiliate Programs

### Program Types

**1. Amazon Associates**:
- Commission: 1-10%
- Products: Everything on Amazon
- Easy to join

**2. Software Affiliates**:
- Commission: 20-50%
- Products: Software, tools, courses
- High commissions

**3. Course Affiliates**:
- Commission: 30-50%
- Products: Online courses
- High rates

---

## Creating Affiliate Content

### Content Types That Convert

**1. Product Reviews**: Honest reviews, pros/cons
**2. Comparison Videos**: Product A vs. Product B
**3. Tutorial Videos**: How to use product
**4. "Best Of" Lists**: Top 5/10 products
**5. Case Studies**: How you used product

---

### Using [OpenVideo Editor](/)

**Method 1: URL to Video**:
- Paste product URL
- Generate script from URL
- Create video
- Add affiliate link
- Publish

**Method 2: Custom Script**:
- Write review script
- Create video
- Add affiliate link
- Publish

**Pro Tip**: URL to Video = fastest affiliate content creation.

---

## Conversion Optimization

### How to Increase Conversions

**1. Build Trust First**: Provide value before promoting
**2. Be Honest**: Real experience, pros/cons
**3. Show Value**: Demonstrate benefits
**4. Clear CTAs**: Specific call-to-action
**5. Track and Optimize**: Monitor click rates

**Target**: 1-5% conversion rate is good

---

## Affiliate Link Placement

**1. Video Description**: Primary placement
**2. Video Itself**: Mention in video
**3. Pinned Comment**: Always visible
**4. End Screen**: Visual CTA

**Pro Tip**: Use all placements for maximum conversions.

---

## Scaling Strategy

### Phase 1: Setup (Week 1)
- Join 5 affiliate programs
- Get affiliate links
- Create first video

### Phase 2: Testing (Weeks 2-4)
- Create 10 affiliate videos
- Test different products
- Track conversions

### Phase 3: Scaling (Month 2+)
- Double down on winners
- Create more of what works
- Scale profitable income

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Start with products you use** = authentic
2. **Focus on high-value products** = higher commission
3. **Create comparison content** = multiple links
4. **Build email list** = better conversions
