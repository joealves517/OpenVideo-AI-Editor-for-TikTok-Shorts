# Lesson 6: Content Calendar & Planning

**Duration**: 30 minutes  
**Goal**: Plan and organize your content for consistent posting

---

## Why Content Planning Matters

**The Problem Without Planning**:
- Inconsistent posting
- Last-minute content creation
- Missed opportunities
- Stress and burnout
- Poor quality

**The Solution: Content Calendar**:
- Plan months in advance
- Consistent posting
- Better quality
- Less stress
- More opportunities

**Planning can increase consistency by 5x and reduce stress by 90%.**

---

## Content Calendar Basics

### What It Is

**Definition**: Visual schedule of all your content

**Benefits**:
- See what's publishing when
- Plan content themes
- Avoid gaps
- Maintain consistency
- Better organization

**Tools**:
- [OpenVideo Editor Content Calendar](/)
- Google Calendar
- Spreadsheet
- Content planning apps

---

### Types of Calendars

**1. Monthly Calendar**:
- See entire month
- Plan themes
- Identify gaps
- Schedule content

**2. Weekly Calendar**:
- Detailed weekly plan
- Daily content
- Time-specific
- More granular

**3. Platform-Specific**:
- Separate calendars per platform
- Platform optimization
- Different strategies
- Cross-promotion

**Pro Tip**: Use [OpenVideo Editor's Content Calendar](/) for integrated planning.

## Planning Strategy

### Strategy 1: Theme-Based Planning

**How It Works**:
- Plan monthly themes
- Weekly sub-themes
- Daily content supports themes
- Cohesive content strategy

**Example**:
- **Month**: AI Tools for Creators
- **Week 1**: AI Script Writing
- **Week 2**: AI Video Creation
- **Week 3**: AI Automation
- **Week 4**: AI Optimization

**Benefits**:
- Cohesive content
- Builds authority
- Better SEO
- Series potential

---

### Strategy 2: Content Mix Planning

**How It Works**:
- Mix different content types
- Balance educational, entertainment, promotional
- Variety keeps audience engaged
- Test what works

**Example Weekly Mix**:
- **Monday**: Educational (How-to)
- **Tuesday**: Tips (Quick tips)
- **Wednesday**: Entertainment (Fun facts)
- **Thursday**: Case Study (Success story)
- **Friday**: Promotional (Product review)

**Benefits**:
- Audience variety
- Test different formats
- Avoid monotony
- Better engagement

---

### Strategy 3: Evergreen + Trending Mix

**How It Works**:
- 70% evergreen content
- 30% trending content
- Balance long-term and short-term
- Sustainable strategy

**Evergreen Examples**:
- How-to tutorials
- Educational content
- Tips and strategies
- Product reviews

**Trending Examples**:
- Current events
- Viral topics
- Seasonal content
- News updates

**Benefits**:
- Long-term traffic
- Short-term spikes
- Balanced strategy
- Sustainable growth

---

## Monthly Planning Process

### Step 1: Review Last Month (30 minutes)

**What to Review**:
- Top-performing content
- What worked
- What didn't work
- Engagement patterns
- Revenue performance

**Questions to Ask**:
- What got most views?
- What got most engagement?
- What converted best?
- What should I do more of?
- What should I stop doing?

**Result**: Data-driven planning for next month.

---

### Step 2: Research Topics (1 hour)

**How to Research**:
1. Use [OpenVideo Editor Idea Discovery](/)
2. Check trending topics
3. Analyze competitor content
4. Review saved topics
5. Identify gaps

**What to Find**:
- 30-60 topics for the month
- Mix of evergreen and trending
- Variety of formats
- High-demand topics

**Result**: Month of content ideas ready.

---

### Step 3: Create Content Calendar (30 minutes)

**How to Create**:
1. Set up calendar (monthly view)
2. Assign topics to days
3. Plan content mix
4. Schedule optimal times
5. Add themes

**Tools**:
- [OpenVideo Editor Content Calendar](/)
- Google Calendar
- Spreadsheet
- Planning app

**Result**: Visual plan for entire month.

---

### Step 4: Batch Create Content (2 hours)

**Using Bulk Scheduler**:
1. Open Bulk Scheduler in [OpenVideo Editor](/)
2. Enter topics from calendar
3. Configure settings
4. Generate all videos
5. Schedule automatically

**Result**: Month of content created and scheduled.

---

## Weekly Planning Process

### Sunday: Planning Day (1 hour)

**Tasks**:
1. Review week ahead
2. Check suggested topics
3. Plan 7-10 videos
4. Schedule in calendar
5. Prepare for week

**Result**: Week planned, ready to execute.

---

### Monday-Friday: Execution (5-10 min/day)

**Daily Tasks**:
1. Check calendar
2. Create scheduled videos
3. Engage with comments
4. Monitor performance
5. Adjust if needed

**Result**: Consistent daily execution.

---

### Saturday: Review (30 minutes)

**Tasks**:
1. Review week's performance
2. Identify what worked
3. Plan improvements
4. Prepare for next week

**Result**: Continuous improvement.
