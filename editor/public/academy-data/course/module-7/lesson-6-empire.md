# Lesson 6: Building a Content Empire

**Duration**: 30 minutes  
**Goal**: Build a sustainable, scalable content business

---

## The Content Empire Vision

**What It Is**:
- Multiple channels
- Multiple revenue streams
- Systems and automation
- Team support
- Sustainable growth

**The Goal**: $10,000+/month, scalable, sustainable

---

## Empire Building Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-6)
- 1-3 channels
- 2-3 revenue streams
- Systems in place
- $1,000-3,000/month

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 7-12)
- 3-5 channels
- All revenue streams
- Team building
- $3,000-10,000/month

### Phase 3: Empire (Months 12+)
- 5+ channels
- All revenue streams active
- Full team
- $10,000+/month
