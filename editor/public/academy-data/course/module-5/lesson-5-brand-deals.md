# Lesson 5: Video Sponsorships & Brand Deals

**Duration**: 25 minutes  
**Goal**: Land video sponsorships and brand deals for premium income

---

## Why Video Sponsorships Matter

**The Opportunity**:
- Highest per-deal revenue
- $100-10,000+ per video
- Premium rates
- Scalable income

**The Reality**:
- Formula: ~$100 per 1,000 subscribers
- Higher for engaged audiences
- Recurring opportunities
- Can be main income source

**Video sponsorships can become your highest-paying revenue stream.**

---

## Understanding Video Sponsorships

### What They Are

**Definition**: Brands pay you to promote their products/services in your videos

**Types**:
- Sponsored videos (full video promotion)
- Product placements (natural integration)
- Brand mentions (brief mentions)
- Long-term collaborations (multiple videos)

**Why Brands Pay**:
- Reach your audience
- Credibility from you
- Targeted marketing
- Better ROI than ads

---

### Earning Potential

**By Subscriber Count**:
- 1,000 subscribers: $100-500 per video
- 10,000 subscribers: $1,000-5,000 per video
- 100,000 subscribers: $10,000-50,000 per video

**By Engagement**:
- High engagement = higher rates
- Engaged audience = more valuable
- Better conversion = premium rates

**By Niche**:
- High-value niches = higher rates
- Business, finance = premium
- Technology = good rates
- All niches = opportunities

**Pro Tip**: Focus on engagement - it's worth more than just subscribers.

---

## How to Get Video Sponsorships

### Method 1: Brands Reach Out

**When It Happens**:
- Once you have audience
- Brands find you
- Through platforms
- Via email/DMs

**Requirements**:
- Established channel
- Engaged audience
- Professional content
- Good metrics

**Timeline**: Usually starts at 5,000-10,000 subscribers

---

### Method 2: Creator Marketplaces

**Platforms**:
- YouTube Creator Marketplace
- TikTok Creator Marketplace
- Instagram Creator Marketplace
- Facebook Brand Collabs Manager

**How It Works**:
1. Join marketplace
2. Create profile
3. Brands browse creators
4. Brands reach out
5. Negotiate deals

**Pro Tip**: Join all marketplaces for maximum opportunities.

---

### Method 3: Reach Out to Brands

**How to Do It**:
1. Identify brands in your niche
2. Research contact info
3. Create pitch
4. Send email
5. Follow up

**Pitch Should Include**:
- Your channel stats
- Audience demographics
- Engagement rates
- Content examples
- Collaboration ideas
- Pricing

**Pro Tip**: Personalize pitches - generic emails get ignored.

---

## Creating Sponsored Video Content

### Best Practices

**1. Be Transparent**:
- Disclose sponsorship
- Follow platform rules
- Builds trust
- Legal requirement

**2. Maintain Authenticity**:
- Only promote products you believe in
- Honest reviews
- Real experience
- Builds credibility

**3. Provide Value**:
- Don't just promote
- Educate about product
- Show use cases
- Deliver value

**4. Integrate Naturally**:
- Don't force promotion
- Natural integration
- Fits your content
- Better performance

**Pro Tip**: Use [OpenVideo Editor](/) to create professional sponsored video content.

## Long-Term Video Partnerships

### Why They're Valuable

**Benefits**:

- Predictable revenue
- Less pitching needed
- Better rates (volume discount)

**How to Get Them**:
- Deliver on first deal
- Exceed expectations
- Build relationship
- Propose long-term partnership

- Predictable income
