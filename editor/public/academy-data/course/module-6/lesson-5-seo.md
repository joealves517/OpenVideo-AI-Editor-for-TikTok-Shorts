# Lesson 5: SEO & Discoverability

**Duration**: 25 minutes  
**Goal**: Optimize content for search and discovery

---

## Why SEO Matters

**The Opportunity**:
- Search traffic = long-term views
- Evergreen content = continuous traffic
- Better rankings = more visibility
- Algorithm favor

**The Impact**:
- SEO-optimized content = 2-3x more views
- Long-term traffic
- Sustainable growth
- Better monetization

**SEO optimization can 2-3x your organic traffic.**

---

## SEO Optimization Strategies

### 1. Keyword Research

**Using OpenVideo Editor's Idea Discovery**:
- Research high-volume keywords
- Find low-competition terms
- Identify trending topics
- Plan content around keywords

**Pro Tip**: Target long-tail keywords (3-5 words) for easier ranking.

---

### 2. Title Optimization

**Best Practices**:
- Primary keyword in first 3 words
- 50-60 characters
- Clear value proposition
- Include numbers when possible

**Example**: "How to Create Faceless Videos in 5 Minutes - Complete Guide"

**Pro Tip**: Use ChatGPT to generate SEO-optimized titles.

---

### 3. Description Optimization

**Best Practices**:
- First 125 characters most important
- Include keywords naturally
- Clear value proposition
- Call-to-action
- Links to resources

**Pro Tip**: Front-load keywords and value.

---

### 4. Tags and Keywords

**Best Practices**:
- 5-10 relevant tags
- Mix broad and specific
- Include variations
- Use Idea Discovery for research

**Pro Tip**: Use tags that match search queries.

---

### 5. Thumbnail Optimization

**Best Practices**:
- Eye-catching visuals
- Clear text overlay
- High contrast
- Brand consistency

**Pro Tip**: Test different thumbnails for best performance.

---

## Content Strategy for SEO

### Strategy 1: Evergreen Content

**What It Is**: Timeless content that stays relevant

**Why It Works**:
- Long-term traffic
- Continuous views
- Better SEO
- Sustainable

**Examples**:
- How-to guides
- Educational content
- Tutorials
- Comprehensive guides

**Pro Tip**: 70% evergreen, 30% trending for balance.

---

### Strategy 2: Long-Form Content

**Why It Works**:
- More keywords
- Better rankings
- Higher watch time
- More ad revenue

**Target**: 10-20 minute videos

**Pro Tip**: Longer content = better SEO.

---

### Strategy 3: Series Content

**Why It Works**:
- Multiple videos on topic
- Better keyword coverage
- Increased watch time
- Cross-linking

**Pro Tip**: Create series for major topics.

---

## Pro Tips

### 1. Use Idea Discovery

**Why**: Real-time data, high-volume keywords

**How**: Research before creating content

**Result**: Better SEO from start.

---

### 2. Optimize Everything

**Strategy**: Title, description, tags, thumbnail

**Why**: All elements affect SEO

**Result**: Maximum optimization.

---

### 3. Track Performance

**Strategy**: Monitor rankings, adjust strategy

**Why**: Data-driven optimization

**Result**: Continuous improvement.
