# Module 1: Foundations of Faceless Video Content

**Duration**: 2 hours  
**Goal**: Understand the fundamentals and choose your niche

## Module Overview

In this module, you'll learn:
- What faceless video content really is
- Why this business model works
- The economics of faceless content
- How to choose your niche (top 12 paying niches)
- Market research and validation
- Setting up your first channel

**Outcome**: You'll have a clear niche, validated market, and ready-to-go channel.
