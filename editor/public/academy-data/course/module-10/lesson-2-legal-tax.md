# Lesson 2: Legal & Tax Considerations

**Duration**: 30 minutes  
**Goal**: Understand legal and tax requirements

---

## Legal Considerations

### 1. Business Structure

**Sole Proprietorship**: Simple, no protection
**LLC**: Protection, still simple
**Corporation**: Advanced, more protection

**Recommendation**: LLC for protection

---

### 2. Contracts

**When Needed**:
- Brand deals
- Team members
- Partnerships
- Services

**Action**: Use templates or hire lawyer

---

### 3. Intellectual Property

**Protect**:
- Your content
- Brand name
- Logos
- Products

**Action**: Understand your rights

---

## Tax Considerations

### 1. Track Income

**What to Track**:
- All revenue streams
- AdSense
- Affiliates
- Products
- Brand deals

**Action**: Track monthly

---

### 2. Track Expenses

**What to Track**:
- Software & API key costs (if any)
- Equipment
- Domain & Web Hosting
- Team costs
- Business expenses

**Action**: Track all business expenses

---

### 3. Quarterly Taxes

**When**: If earning $1,000+/quarter

**Action**: Set aside 25-30% for taxes

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Consult professional** = proper setup
2. **Track everything** = easier taxes
3. **Set aside taxes** = no surprises
4. **Understand requirements** = compliance

---

## Action Items

- [ ] Consult tax professional
- [ ] Set up tracking system
- [ ] Understand requirements
- [ ] Set aside for taxes
- [ ] Keep records

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Legal structure** = protection
2. **Track income/expenses** = easier taxes
3. **Set aside taxes** = no surprises
4. **Consult professional** = proper setup
