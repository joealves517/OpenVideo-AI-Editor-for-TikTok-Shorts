# Lesson 5: Exit Strategies

**Duration**: 25 minutes  
**Goal**: Understand options for exiting your business

---

## Exit Options

### 1. Sell Your Channel

**When**: Established, profitable channel

**Value**: 20-50x monthly revenue

**Process**: Use broker, negotiate, transfer

---

### 2. Keep and Maintain

**When**: Passive income, sustainable

**Strategy**: Minimal maintenance, continue earning

**Result**: Long-term passive income

---

### 3. Pass to Team

**When**: Team can run it

**Strategy**: Train team, step back

**Result**: Business continues without you

---

## Building Value

### What Increases Value

**Metrics**:
- Revenue
- Growth rate
- Audience size
- Engagement
- Diversified revenue

**Systems**:
- Documented processes
- Team in place
- Automated systems
- Scalable model

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Build value** = better exit options
2. **Document systems** = easier transfer
3. **Diversify revenue** = higher value
4. **Plan ahead** = better exit
