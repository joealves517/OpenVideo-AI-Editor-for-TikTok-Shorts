## Optimization Strategies

### 1. Improve Retention

**How**:
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Deliver value throughout
- Use storytelling
- Clear structure

**Result**: Higher retention = better performance

### 2. Increase Engagement

**How**:

- Respond to comments
- Community posts
- Live streams

**Result**: Higher engagement = algorithm favor

### 3. Optimize SEO

**How**:

- Optimize titles
- Better descriptions
- Relevant tags

**Result**: More organic traffic
